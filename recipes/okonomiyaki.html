Recipe for the Legendary Okonomiyaki

Description

Okonomiyaki (お好み焼き) is sometimes called “Japanese savory pancake” or “Japanese pizza”, but personally, I think it is more like a dish between savory pancake and frittata.It is made with flour, eggs, tempura scraps (tenkasu), cabbage, and pork belly slices and topped with a variety of condiments like okonomiyaki sauce, Japanese mayonnaise, dried seaweed, and dried bonito flakes

Ingredients

  • Baking powder
  • Beaten egg whites
  • Well drained tofu
  • Grated taro
  • Well-drained potatoes
  • Grated lotus root

Recipe tips

  1. Shed the cabbage finely
  2. Do not pack it down too much
  3. Eat it right away
